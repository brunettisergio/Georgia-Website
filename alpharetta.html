ALPHARETTA

Alpharetta is a city in northern Fulton County, Georgia, United States, founded in 1,858, is a part of the Atlanta metropolitan area. As of the 2020 US Census, Alpharetta's population was 65,818. Alpharetta is a classic Atlanta suburb, and is well known for being one of the richest cities in Georgia.

/////////////

Education

The city is served by Fulton County Schools, which is a school district headquartered in Sandy Springs, Georgia, United States. It is the fourth-largest school system in Georgia and the only non-contiguous school district in the state, having a 17-mile (27 km) separation (Atlanta Public Schools) between the north and south.
